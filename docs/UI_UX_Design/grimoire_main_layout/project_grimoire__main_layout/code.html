Magic System: The Weave

folder Parent
Lore & Magic
calendar_today Created
Oct 24, 2023

The Weave is the underlying fabric of reality that magic users tap into. It is not an infinite resource, but rather a renewable energy field generated by the planet's core and the alignment of the three moons.

The Three Pillars

  • Creation (Aethel): The manipulation of matter and form. Used for construction, healing, and growth.
  • Destruction (Neth): The entropy of energy. Used for combat, decay, and releasing bound potential.
  • Alteration (Vex): The bending of natural laws. Used for illusion, time perception, and gravity.

"To touch the Weave is to invite chaos into the soul. One must be a vessel of iron, not glass."
— Archmage Valerius

Access to the Weave requires a Conduit. Without a conduit, raw magic burns the caster from the inside out, a phenomenon known as "Arcane Scorch".
