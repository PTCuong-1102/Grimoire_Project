Chapter 4: The Iron Gate

The Unexpected Guest

The wind howled through the cracks of the stone walls, carrying the scent of rain and old iron. Elias Thorne stood by the window, his silhouette cut sharply against the gray light of the dawn. He hadn't slept in days. The weight of the Iron Citadel pressed upon his mind like a physical burden.

"You shouldn't be here," a voice whispered from the shadows.

Elias didn't turn. He knew the voice. He knew the danger. But for the first time in ten years, he felt something other than regret. He felt a spark of the forbidden magic stirring in his veins.
